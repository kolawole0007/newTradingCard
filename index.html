A Rabbit

Rabbits, also known as bunnies or bunny rabbits, are small mammals in the family Leporidae (which also includes the hares), which is in the order Lagomorpha (which also includes the pikas). A male rabbit is called a buck; a female is called a doe, while rabbit once referred only to the young animals.Another term for a young rabbit is bunny.

Scientific Name Oryctolagus cuniculus.
length 7.9 inches
Lifespan 8 to 12 years
Habitat Open areas,Forests and woodlands

Rabbit's ears aren't just for hearing which is excellent, Their large ears can rotate a full 180 degrees, helping them pinpoint sounds from any direction.
